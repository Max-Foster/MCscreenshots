Bad Graphical is a small, 2 person game development team (my brother and I). I like coding, and trying to make video games. But I'm not very good at it, so there will be no AAA games or anything like that. I really like old video games, and their bad graphics. So why not make more of them? So that's why this is called Bad Graphical. Because most of my games will have bad graphics (deliberately or not). I hope you enjoy the games to some extent, and please give me feedback so I can fix bugs or improve the games. Thanks.
